How to play

  • Each puzzle shows a grid with pairs of coloured dots.
  • Draw a path from each dot to its matching colour.
  • Paths cannot cross each other.
  • Connect all pairs to solve the level.

Controls

  • Draw — click or tap a dot and drag.
  • Retract — drag back along your own path to undo.
  • Restart a colour — start drawing from either of its dots again.

Tips

  • Think about which paths have the fewest possible routes first.
  • Constrained dots (near corners or edges) are a good place to start.
